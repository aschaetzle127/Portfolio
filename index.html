About me

I like to break things and make things.

My name is Alex Schaetzle, and I am a Full Stack Development student at the University of Arizona. Some say I have always been an engineer at heart, as my whole life has been spent finding the most efficient ways to break things and then, of course, put them back together. This mindset, along with my creative heart, led me into the fast paced world of retail in many different "back end" facets.

After many years in the creative industry of Merchandise and Operations Management, I recently found a passion for coding and developing, which sent my life into a new and exciting direction. I am now currently working on several projects as a student and cannot wait to share my passion, ambition, and skills with a company when my program ends.
